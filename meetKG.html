Our Love Story

Glenn and Katie were on a debate team together in high school. Glenn and Katie met at a debate tournament December 11th, 2010. Glenn had a crush on Katie in high school. Katie invited the whole debate team to come watch her soccer game. Only Glenn showed up. It was cold and rainy day and they shared a jacket to keep warm. Glenn attended many more of her soccer games before he finally worked up the courage to ask her to be his girlfriend. They started dating April 30th, 2012. Glenn attended Mizzou the following year and Katie moved away to Northwest. Even though they lived hours apart they couldn't stay away from each other. On July 16th, 2016 Glenn got on one knee in front of friends and family at the JC Nichols fountain in KC to ask Katie to marry him. Now they are asking you to attend their long awaited wedding July 7th, 2018.
